## Research Interests

My broad area of research is Partial Differential Equations. Specifically, I focus on the mathematical fields of homogenization and optimal control problems.

## PhD

*   PhD from Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India.
*   Research Supervisor: [Prof. A. K Nandakumaran](https://math.iisc.ac.in/~nands/)
*   Thesis Title: Homogenization of Partial Differential Equation on oscillating boundary domains with $L^1$ data and Optimal Control Problems

## Research Positions

*   Feb 2023 - Nov 2023: Research Associate, Dept. of Mathematics, IISc Bangalore.
*   Dec 2023 - Till Date: Post Doctoral Research Fellow, Stat Math Unit, ISI Bangalore.
